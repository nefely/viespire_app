How It Works

Only a few taps between a static image and a masterpiece. It has never been simpler:

  • Set the movement patterns for the picture. Give life to what was lifeless before.

  • See the animation in its glory. Enjoy the masterpiece you have just created.

  • Edit the animation and borders in any way you like. Don’t stop until it’s perfect.

  • Adjust how fast the animation runs. Aim for realism or surreal scenes - it’s up to you.

  • Browse the library of special effects. Choose any to boost the magic of your image.

  • Add objects to make the image even better. Scale, move, rotate them in any way.
